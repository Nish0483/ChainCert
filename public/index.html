ABOUT US

ChainCert is a startup platform that provides services for entities involved in issuing certificates. We leverage the power of blockchain technology to ensure the security, immutability, and transparency of certification processes.

At ChainCert, we employ the robust keccak256 algorithm to encrypt certificate details, ensuring the highest level of data protection making it impossible to tamper with or modify once issued.Our input checks also make sure no duplicate data is entered

We extend a warm invitation to entities of all kinds to utilize our services and join us in securing and transparently managing their certification processes.
